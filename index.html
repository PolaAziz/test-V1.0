Nursery Policy & Parent Guidelines

(September 2025 – September 2026)

1. Registration

  • Parents must fill in an application form from the nursery administration.
  • Application fee:
    • Languages section: 160 EGP
  • Required documents after acceptance:
    • Copy of the child's birth certificate
    • Copy of baptism certificate
    • 2 personal photos of the child
    • Copy of mother's ID and father's ID
    • Photo of the person authorized to pick up the child

2. Monthly Fees

  • Languages section: 950 EGP per month (without meals)
  • Meals (optional): +700 EGP per month
  • Arabic section: 600 EGP per month (without meals)
  • Meals (optional): +500 EGP per month
  • Meals program is supervised by Dr. Irene Halim (Nutrition Specialist) and includes breakfast + lunch.
  • Meals must be paid before the 25th of the previous month.
  • Meal prices are reviewed every 3 months.

3. Reservation and Discounts

  • To reserve a child's place during absence: pay 50% of the monthly fee.
  • If no reservation is paid, the child loses the place and must re-apply.
  • If the child is absent for more than 2 months, they will be placed on a waiting list.
  • Sibling discount: 10% for the second child (applies only to the basic fee).

4. Hosting Service

  • Hosting during nursery hours:
    • Without meal: 45 EGP/day
    • With meal: 85 EGP/day
  • Extra hours: 30 EGP per hour

5. Late Pickup Penalties

  • 15 minutes late: 30 EGP
  • 30 minutes late: 40 EGP
  • 45 minutes late: 50 EGP
  • 1 hour late: 60 EGP
  • Fines increase if lateness is repeated.
  • Please respect nursery official timings.

6. Meal Times

  • Breakfast: 9:30 – 10:00 am
  • Lunch: 1:00 – 1:30 pm
  • If a child comes after 10:00 am, breakfast will not be served. Please make sure your child eats at home if late.

7. Communication Policy

  • Daily notes will be written in the follow-up notebook sent with the child.
  • For complaints or questions: contact nursery administration only (WhatsApp: 01118040781).
  • Parents should not contact teachers or staff directly. Communication is only with the branch manager.

8. Health Policy

  • Sick children will not be allowed in nursery for the safety of others.
  • Children with infectious diseases may return only with a doctor's medical report.
  • If a child is sick for more than 2 days, a medical report is required.
  • Parents must inform the nursery in writing of any allergies or chronic illnesses.

9. Reports and Follow-up

  • Daily report about the child's activities.
  • Progress report (behavioral and educational) every 2 months.
  • Parents should read and follow up on all notes.

10. General Rules

  • Children must wear the nursery uniform (except Tuesday – casual clothes allowed).
  • Parents must inform the nursery in case of travel or long absence.
  • Please take care of books and notebooks.
  • Bring healthy food only for children not joining the meal program:
    • Allowed: fruits, plain biscuits, milk
    • Not allowed: sweets, candy
  • Hygiene:
    • Cut nails regularly
    • Keep hair clean
    • Wash food box and water bottle daily
  • Reduce screen time (TV/phone) at home.
  • Parents must provide monthly supplies:
    • Tissue box
    • Wet wipes
    • Towel with the child's name
    • Crocs for indoor use
    • Change of clothes with name
    • Personal cup
    • Hair brush
